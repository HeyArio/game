Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

Quorum (“we”, “us”) is a free daily browser game at quorumdaily.com. This policy explains what we collect, why, and the choices you have. We keep data collection to the minimum needed to run the game.

What we collect

  • Account information — when you sign in with Google, we receive your name, email address, and profile picture from Google’s OAuth service. We never see or store your Google password.
  • Gameplay data — your daily picks, confidence, XP, streak, league standing, quests, and referrals, so the game can save your progress across devices.
  • Device storage — we use your browser’s local storage for small items like onboarding state and a pending invite code. These are not advertising cookies.
  • Anonymous usage analytics — if enabled, we use a privacy-friendly, cookieless analytics tool to count page views and key actions in aggregate. It does not track you across other sites or build a personal profile.

Why we use it

  • To run the game: save your progress, compute scores and leaderboards, and credit invites.
  • To understand, in aggregate, how the game is used so we can improve it.
  • To keep the game secure and prevent abuse (for example, server-side validation of scores).

Who processes your data

  • Supabase — our database, authentication, and backend host. Your account and gameplay data are stored there.
  • Google — provides sign-in (OAuth). See Google’s own privacy policy for how they handle your account.
  • Analytics provider — if enabled, receives anonymous, aggregate usage events only.

We do not sell your personal data, and we don’t share it with advertisers.

Data retention & your choices

We keep your account and gameplay data while your account exists. You can request access to, correction of, or deletion of your data at any time by emailing us at the address below — we’ll delete your profile and gameplay records on request.

Children

Quorum is intended for adults and is not directed at children under 13. We don’t knowingly collect data from children under 13.

Changes

We may update this policy as the game evolves. We’ll revise the “last updated” date above when we do.

Contact

Questions or data requests: info@nazarbanai.com.
